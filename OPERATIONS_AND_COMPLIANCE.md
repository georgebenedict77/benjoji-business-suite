# Benjoji Business Suite Operations And Compliance Notes

This suite includes practical operational controls for authentication, receipts, backups, payment routing, and staff access, but every client business should still review local legal and regulatory requirements with qualified advisors before going live.

## Included Operational Safeguards

- Password-based account access with optional second-stage PIN policy
- Owner-only control center for business profile, payment routing, and security policy
- Account lockout rules for repeated failed logins
- Local backup snapshots with owner-driven restore workflow
- Receipt policy text and compliance acknowledgement records
- Payment method enable/disable controls per business

## Still Required Before Live Deployment

- Real payment-provider agreements and API credentials for M-Pesa, Airtel Money, banks, or card processors
- Local tax, privacy, employment, and consumer-protection review for the target country
- Printer and hardware testing for the specific business environment
- Disaster-recovery drills on the target workstation or branch setup
- Final user acceptance testing with the actual cashier and owner workflow

## Data Responsibility

Each business using the suite is responsible for:

- protecting device and account access
- reviewing retained customer and payment data
- defining lawful receipt, privacy, and refund wording
- validating backup practices and recovery frequency
- ensuring payment routing matches their registered business channels
