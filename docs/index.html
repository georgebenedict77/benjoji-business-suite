Core Modules

One suite for selling, controlling stock, protecting operations, and reviewing performance.

Point of Sale

Basket-first checkout, split payments, popup tender flow, receipt generation, and protected cashier actions.

Inventory Management

Products, stock in, stock out, low stock visibility, product codes, and stock movement records from one panel.

Reports and Accounting

Daily, weekly, monthly, and annual summaries with drill-down views into sales, activity, and payment usage.

Debt and Repayments

Track partial payments, outstanding balances, and debt settlement history without losing transaction context.

Workspace Control Center

Manage branding, receipt rules, payment routes, security policy, compliance notes, and recovery settings.

Independent Workspaces

Each business gets separate owners, staff, branding, backups, and data so client operations never mix.

Why It Stands Out

Designed as a sellable business platform, not a one-company tool.

Benjoji Business Suite is structured so any business can create its own workspace, customize its brand, define its payment methods, and control who gets access. The public identity stays with the suite, while each client business shapes its own workspace experience.

Independent branding Every client business can use its own name, logo, contacts, and receipt profile.
Operational resilience Built-in backups, restore workflow, workspace isolation, and owner-controlled sensitive actions.
Professional workflow Cashier, inventory, reporting, and control flows are organized to feel clean and practical.
Independent workspaces 1 per business
